## Project Overview
This Power BI Dashboard provides a detailed analysis of imported vehicles in Saudi Arabia during the third quarter of 2024. The project focuses on market trends, manufacturing performance, and consumer segmentation (Individual vs. Corporate), offering a clear view of the automotive import landscape.

## Key Insights
**Market Scale:** Analysis of 261 vehicle models from 60 different manufacturers.

**Top & Bottom Performers:** Identification of the top 3 most profitable manufacturers and the highest-selling vehicle models.

**Financial Summary:** Tracking total manufacturing prices for both corporate and individual sectors (Totaling over 100M).

**Consumer Segmentation:** A balanced view of the market split between Individuals (50.13%) and Companies (49.87%).

## Tech Stack
Power BI: Advanced data visualization and slicer integration.

Power Query: Data cleaning and merging of manufacturer and model datasets.

DAX: Calculation of profit margins and sales totals.

## How to Use
To explore the interactive report:

1- Download the المركبات المستوردة في الربع الثالث لعام 2024 file from this repository.
2- Open the file using Power BI Desktop.
3- Use the Beneficiary Category (فئة المستفيد) slicer to toggle between Corporate and Individual data.
4- Filter by Manufacturing Year (سنة الصنع) to see historical trends.
